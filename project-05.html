The Concept

This is the background video projected on the giant screen at the ribbon-cutting ceremony of ACY Securities Melbourne Branch. The requirement was to convey a "high-tech" feel visually, and the company's logo needed to look imposing at the end.

High technology is a relatively abstract concept, to achieve this, I utilized geometric shapes, futuristic elements, gorgeous images to convey the concept of high-tech and edited them into a looping video.
